My Strategy

Every year has a trend in Design

Take inspiration from the most popular existing app and web designs while also considering the design trends for this year. However, this doesn't mean you should lack originality in your work. It's important to be aware of the design conventions that users are already familiar with because they use existing apps. By incorporating familiar elements and staying current with the latest trends, I can make your design more user-friendly and intuitive.

Use common UI components

Users are more likely to understand how to use your product when it follows familiar patterns. Moreover, this approach benefits not only users but also developers, as it simplifies the implementation process. As a UI/UX designer, I prioritize both user and developer needs, ensuring a harmonious experience for all parties involved.

The UX Writer

We will strive to create shorter text within our product design. I believe that 89% of users prefer not to read lengthy content. What we aim to do is find ways to directly address their goals.
